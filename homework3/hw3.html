Part 1

Calculates a student's final grade based on weighted averages of homework, midterm exam, final exam, and participation scores. When the submit button is clicked, it validates that all inputs are numbers between 0 and 100, displaying an error if not. The final average is computed using specific weightings (50% homework, 20% midterm, 20% final, 10% participation) and rounded to the nearest whole number. A letter grade (A-F) is assigned based on the average, and the results are displayed in a designated output area. If the grade is D or F, a message prompts the student to retake the course.
